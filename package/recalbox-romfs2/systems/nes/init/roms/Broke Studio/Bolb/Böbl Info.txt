Type: Platformer/exploration

DESCRIPTION
You’re a bubble, water is your friend and solid blocks kill you.

Böbl is a platormer based on water physics and exploraton. You can actvate bells as checkpoints and collect ducks hidden all over the map. Your trip starts in a lost temple. You will move in diferent rooms, fall in the underground, use pipes, dive deep in thewater and go up into the clouds to reach the ending.

Böbl is a short game for NES available as a ROM file. It was made in 1 month for the NESdev compo 2019. Böbl won the 1st place. We invite you to check all the other games of the NESdevcompo 2019.

CONTROLS
Hold A to dive, then release and let buoyancy handle the rest. Let’s fnd all the lost duckies!
