What’s harder than escaping a planet full of dangerous flora and fauna? Doing it all with a crystal-hungry octopus fused to your head!

Run, jump, and dash your way through 12 levels as you search for the missing parts of Debs’s ship to escape the hostile alien planet Vespasian 7MV!
