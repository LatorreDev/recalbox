THE TINIES & COGITO RETURN
==========================

"The Tinies", is a freeware port of the original game (Atreid Concept - 1992 - PC, Macintosh, Atari ST and Amiga) by Antoine VIGNAU and Olivier ZARDINI.

"Cogito Return" is a freeware port of the original game (Atreid Concept - 1992 - PC, Macintosh and Newton) by Antoine VIGNAU and Olivier ZARDINI.

Antoine VIGNAU, Olivier ZARDINI, and several former employees of Atreid Concept, kindly give Recalbox the authorisation to distribute both games.
